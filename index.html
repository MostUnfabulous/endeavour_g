Endeavour

A chilled out semi-hardcore raiding guild on Magtheridon-EU. Currently recruiting awesome, like-minded players for Mythic progression. Scroll down or talk to one of us in-game for more info!

Endeavour go 6/7 HC!

24th October

Not even blizzard's temperamental servers could stop us downing Elerethe, Il'gynoth and Cenarius. Thanks to the focused efforts of our fantastic raid team, in one night we took our guild from 3/7 to 6/7, despite losing an hour of raid time to lag and disconnects. Now we have no doubt we'll achieve 7/7 next week.

Heroic Ursoc and Dragons down!

19th October

We're on our way to greatness - tonight, our devoted raid team cleansed the Dragons of Nightmare and put to rest the great bear spirit Ursoc. We hope you can join us for further progression next week!

Hell, it's about time.

8th October

Looks like we've got ourselves our first heroic kill! Endeavour-Magtheridon downed Nythendra after a perfectly executed attempt on October 2nd 2016. To the victors go the spoils! Join us next week for more progression.
